Help & Support

1

Placing Orders

You can browse food items in the home screen or use the search function. Add items to your cart by proceeding to checkout.

2

Delivery and Tracking

Your order will be picked up by our delivery partners. You can check the current status in the Orders section.

3

Dine-In Services

You can reserve live seats using the Dine-In option. Please arrive at the hotel on time after booking your seat.

4

Refunds and Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled before the hotel accepts them. Refunds are processed within 5-7 working days.

5

Food Donations

Hotels can list excess food for donation, which NGOs and delivery partners can collect and distribute to those in need.

Still need help?

Reach out to our support team at support@foodbridge.com or call 1800-FOOD-BRIDGE.
